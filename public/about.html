Our story
Why Nilecrest exists
Most projects in East Africa need more than one kind of partner: someone to build it, someone to move the materials, and someone to keep the operation running. Nilecrest Holdings was formed so clients don't have to coordinate three different vendors for that. One company, three departments, each run by a manager who knows that trade, with a single point of accountability at the top.
